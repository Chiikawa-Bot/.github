# 🔒 Privacy Policy — Chiikawa Bot

**Last updated:** March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data Chiikawa collects, why, and how it is handled. We are based in the **European Union** and are committed to handling your data responsibly.

---

## 1. Data We Collect

When you use Chiikawa, we may collect and store the following:

| Data Type | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **User IDs** | Identify users for features, moderation, and premium status |
| **Server IDs** | Associate configuration and settings with the correct server |
| **Message Content** | Power features like custom commands, moderation, logging, and the embed builder |

All data is stored in our own **private database.**

---

## 2. Why We Collect This Data

We collect data solely to provide and improve the Bot's functionality. Data is **never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising.** Message content is only processed where required by a specific feature and is not read or monitored manually, except where necessary to investigate abuse or violations of our Terms.

---

## 3. Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as your server has the Bot added, or as long as your account has interacted with a feature that requires storage.

> 📌 We do not currently offer an automated data deletion command. If you wish to have your data removed, please contact us and we will handle it manually.

---

## 4. Third Parties

- **Patreon** — Used to process premium payments. All payment data is handled exclusively by Patreon and subject to their own Privacy Policy. We do not have access to your payment details.
- **Discord** — The Bot operates on Discord's platform and is subject to [Discord's Privacy Policy](https://discord.com/privacy) and Terms of Service.

---

## 5. Your Rights (EU / GDPR)

As we are based in the EU, we acknowledge your rights under the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**, including the right to:

- ✅ Access your personal data
- ✅ Request correction of inaccurate data
- ✅ Request deletion of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly.

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

Chiikawa is not intended for users under the age of **13**. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has used the Bot, please contact us and we will remove their data promptly.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated through our official Discord server. Continued use of the Bot after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

---
